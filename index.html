Who am I?

Web design and me

My choice to study interaction design and web design stems from my years of studying a course called Digital Information Technology (DIT) which i took in all my 5 years at my college. What made me attracted and persistance on taking this course was not only the helpful teacher who i mostly had, but i loved the ability to design and create websites and the very broad requirements for our projects would always excite me as i have always loved the process of coming up with new ways of thinking and ideas, and when im not restricted to specific thoughts/answers, i am able to create better work. This was also the course which i always used to get the highest mark in as compared to all my other courses which would range in which the consistency of my results along with my love for design led me into taking this couse at University.
